FIND YOUR BEST FIT

Computer Vision Engineer

Leverage machine learning algorithms and AI to create systems that can analyze and interpret visual data in sports. A key role in advancing technology for tactical analysis, player performance, and video analysis in football.

Tactical analyst

Study match footage, player movements, and team strategies to provide data-driven insights. Your analysis helps coaches refine game tactics and improve team performance during matches.

Social media manager

Manage the club’s online presence across various platforms. Create engaging content, interact with fans, and ensure the club’s voice reaches millions worldwide through creative digital campaigns.

Physiotherapists

Specialize in preventing, diagnosing, and treating injuries for players. Your role is crucial for recovery and ensuring that athletes perform at their best while maintaining their physical health.

Nutritionists

Design personalized nutrition plans for players to maintain optimal health, energy, and performance. Your expertise supports both recovery and peak physical performance through scientifically-backed dietary recommendations.

Journalist

Report on football-related news, write articles, and conduct interviews with players, coaches, and staff. Your stories bring football to the fans, covering everything from match previews to post-game analyses.
